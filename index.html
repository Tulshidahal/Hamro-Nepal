A 20-day premium journey at a glance

Here’s how we pace an immersive trip. Every day can be tailored — from mountain treks to helicopter add-ons and private workshops.

  1. 01

    Days 1–3 • Kathmandu welcome

    VIP airport arrival, boutique heritage hotel, Old City walking tour, and welcome dinner on a rooftop overlooking Durbar Square.

  2. 02

    Days 4–8 • Himalayan foothills

    Scenic drive to Dhulikhel and Bandipur, guided village hikes, temple blessings, and sunrise yoga with panoramic peaks.

  3. 03

    Days 9–12 • Pokhara lake life

    Private boat cruise, wellness spa treatments, helicopter add-on to Annapurna Base Camp, and sunset dinner on Sarangkot ridge.

  4. 04

    Days 13–16 • Chitwan safari

    Luxe riverfront lodge, private jeep safaris, rhino tracking with naturalists, and Tharu cooking class by lantern light.

  5. 05

    Days 17–20 • Heritage finale

    Return to Kathmandu for artisan workshops, spa day, and farewell tasting menu with live classical music.

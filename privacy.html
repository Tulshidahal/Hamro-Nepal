Privacy Policy

Effective January 2025 • Last updated January 2025

Hamro Vacation (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information shared through our website, email, WhatsApp, and consultation forms.

Information we collect

When you request an itinerary, we collect the details you provide: name, email, phone or WhatsApp number, travel dates, group size, interests, and any notes you submit. We also collect basic analytics (page views, device type) through Google Analytics 4 with anonymized IP addresses.

How we use your information

  • • Crafting custom itineraries, quotes, and travel proposals tailored to your preferences.
  • • Communicating through email, phone, or WhatsApp to refine trip plans and confirm services.
  • • Improving our website experience by analyzing aggregate, de-identified usage trends.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share details with trusted Nepal-based partners (hotels, guides, transport providers) once you authorize us to confirm arrangements.

Data retention & your choices

Inquiry details are retained securely for up to 24 months to facilitate trip planning and follow-up. You may request deletion or updates at any time by contacting us. Marketing emails are sent only with opt-in consent, and every message includes an unsubscribe option.
